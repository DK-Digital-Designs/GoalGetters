Our Culture & Identity

1) Non-Negotiables
  • Maximum effort every session.
  • Respect for teammates, staff, officials, and the game.
  • Accountability for actions on and off the pitch.
  • Discipline in habits, time-keeping, and standards.
  • Passion for the badge and the club.

Players who don‚Äôt align will be moved on, regardless of talent or name.

2) Team Above Individuals (Brotherhood)
  • Ego is out; the collective is everything.
  • No special treatment.
  • Everyone defends, presses, and works for the team.
3) Winning Mentality
  • Losing is not accepted, even in training.
  • Competitiveness is fierce but respectful.
  • We don‚Äôt play for top four; we play to win.
4) Clear Identity of Play
  • Possession-based but intense, vertical, and fast.
  • Players must be intelligent and adaptable.
  • Strong emphasis on tactical discipline and positional play.
5) Youth Development
  • Young players are trusted with responsibility.
  • Development and standards are high from day one.
6) Emotional Connection & Purpose
  • We play with heart and give fans something to believe in.
7) Relentless Standards
  • Players are detailed about diet, recovery, and focus.
  • Training is intense and personalised.
  • We chase progression even in victory.

Mission, Values, Philosophy

Provide a clear pathway from grassroots to competitive regional football. Development comes first; results follow.

Safeguarding & Consent

Parental Consent

Players under 18 train and appear in media only with guardian consent. Our registration process and app both include clear consent steps.

Data Handling

We store only essential information for scheduling, communication, and development tracking. Data is not sold or shared outside football-related activities.
